Our Mission

We are committed to supporting mental health and emotional well-being through compassionate, accessible, and client-centered counseling services. Our mission is to empower individuals, families, and communities to grow emotionally, build resilience, and improve quality of life.

About

Ms. Elen Renata Gomes Pereira is a licensed psychologist with a Bachelor’s in Psychology from UNIP, São Paulo. She provides compassionate counseling and therapeutic care for children, adolescents, and adults, offering individual sessions, group support, and creative workshops that promote mental health, emotional growth, and overall well-being.

Our Services

Individual Counseling

Personalized support for emotional well-being, stress, anxiety, relationships, and life challenges.

Therapeutic Services for Neurodivergent Children

Specialized, play-based, and developmentally appropriate therapeutic support focused on emotional regulation, social skills, communication, and family support.

Support Groups

Safe and supportive group spaces that encourage connection and emotional growth.

Creative Arts Programs

Therapeutic workshops using art to promote self-expression and emotional balance.

Sports-Based Psychosocial Activities

Activities designed to build confidence, teamwork, and mental well-being through movement and play.

Mental Health Workshops

Educational workshops focused on awareness, self-care, and emotional development.
