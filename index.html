Our Mission

We are committed to supporting mental health and emotional well-being through compassionate, accessible, and client-centered counseling services. Our mission is to empower individuals, families, and communities to grow emotionally, build resilience, and improve quality of life.

About

Ms. Elen Renata Gomes Pereira is a licensed psychologist with a Bachelor’s in Psychology from UNIP, São Paulo.

She provides compassionate counseling and therapeutic care for children, adolescents, and adults, offering individual sessions, group support, and creative workshops that promote mental health, emotional growth, and overall well-being.
